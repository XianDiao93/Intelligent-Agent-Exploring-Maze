# Intelligent-Agent-Exploring-Maze
## Basic experiment setting: 15 * 15 map, infinite energy, robot fully knows the whole structure of maze, robots are classified as reactive and memory-based, 20 for each robots for each experiment.

## Experiment1: test performance of two different robots in three knowledge conditions: fully knows the whole structure of maze, only knows the direction of goal, knows nothing.

## Experiment2: test performance of two different robots in three energy conditions: infinite energy, finite energy with light source, finite energy with no light source.

## Experiment3: test performance of two different robots in different scale of maps.

## Evaluation content: completiong rate, average completion time of all completed robots.
